Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Wenky Edge, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment details. We may also collect non-personal data like browser type and device info for analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send order updates and promotions
  • Improve our website and services
  • Enhance your user experience

4. Cookies

We use cookies to personalize content, track your activity, and improve functionality. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share your data with trusted third parties only when necessary (e.g., payment gateways, shipping partners) to fulfill services.

6. Data Security

We implement secure technologies and procedures to protect your personal data. However, no method is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you'd like to make any changes, contact us at wenkyedge@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at wenkyedge@gmail.com.
